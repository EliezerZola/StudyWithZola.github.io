We are here to help you start your studies in Thailand with one of the best private universities in Thailand.
Kasem Bundit University, founded in 1987 by Mr. Kasem Suwandee, is ranked among the top ten private universities in Thailand. The university has two campuses: one on Pattanakarn Road and the other on Romklao Road. Kasem Bundit University offers over 50 academic programs and has more than 12,000 undergraduate students and 500 graduate students. The university also hosts around 700 international students from countries such as Cambodia, China, Congo, India, Italy, Myanmar, Nigeria, Russia, Turkey, Ukraine, the USA, and Vietnam..

Our students are prepared for a multitude of exciting career paths. Whether you aspire to be a business analyst, airline manager, hotel manager, communications specialist, product designer, civil engineer, software developer, senior manager, or engineering manager, these programs equip you with the skills and knowledge to excel. KBU fosters a dynamic learning environment where you can explore your passions, develop your talents, and make a significant impact in your chosen field, ensuring a fulfilling and successful career journey.
